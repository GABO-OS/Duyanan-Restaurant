1. METHODOLOGY & DATA SOURCES
------------------------------
The Projected Revenue (Next Month) is calculated dynamically using the
Holt-Winters Additive Method (Triple Exponential Smoothing).

Instead of applying a single flat growth percentage to the daily average,
the Holt-Winters algorithm forecasts the sales for each of the next 30 days
individually, incorporating trend and weekly seasonality.

The final Projected Revenue is the sum of these 30 individual daily forecasts:

   Projected Revenue = Sum(Forecast Day 1 to 30)
